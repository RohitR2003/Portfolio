    Project Trojan

    Trojan is a linear stealth action-adventure game featuring a time manipulation component that allows players to slow, pause, or rewind time to outsmart enemies and solve puzzles.


    Built completely using C++ and Blueprints in Unreal Engine 5, Trojan leverages your gameplay programming and engine-level expertise. The project focuses on AI perception, custom movement systems, and an immersive environment.


    All assets, including hard-surface models, animations, and PBR textures, were made using Blender, Cascadeur, and Substance Painter respectively.


    The project is optimized for performance and modularity, making it scalable and easily expandable for future levels or features.

    Ghost Mode

    In Ghost Mode, players can record their actions for a limited time, then rewind to the moment recording began. A clone is spawned to replay the recorded sequence exactly, allowing for advanced strategies like diversions, multi-path puzzles, or synchronized takedowns—all while the player takes a new route unnoticed.
