    Architectural Visualization

    This real-time architectural visualization project, built in Unreal Engine 5, allows users to explore interior spaces from a top-down edit mode, a first-person walkthrough, or even in VR.


    In edit mode, users can move furniture, swap out models, and change surface materials. It’s a fully interactive, customizable space designed for architecture previews and virtual staging.


    Assets were created using Blender, Substance Painter, and Quixel Megascans, with all interactions and transitions powered by Blueprints and C++.
